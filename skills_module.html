Learning Objectives Timeline

Introduction to Professional Skills

LO1
  • Define employability skills
  • Identify skills necessary for employment
  • Classify workplace skills
  • Understand values, beliefs, attitudes, and character

CV Writing and Cover Letters

LO2
  • Identify the structure of a CV and cover letter
  • Recognize appropriate registers in cover letter writing
  • Distinguish between CV and résumé
  • Write a cover letter in response to a job advertisement
  • Compose a professional CV

Interview Skills

LO3
  • Identify the basic components of interviews
  • Demonstrate effective interview skills
  • Conduct mock interviews

Negotiation Skills

LO4
  • Examine different types of negotiation skills
  • Understand the importance of negotiation skills for professional development

Introduction to Portfolio Writing

LO5
  • Classify different types of portfolios
  • Write a self-reflective portfolio
  • Create a professional portfolio (Assignment)

Team Leadership Skills

LO6
  • Understand team development stages
  • Apply team leadership strategies
  • Problem-solving and decision-making
  • Embrace diversity in teams, organizations, and society

Facilitating and Conducting Meetings

LO7
  • Develop writing skills for meetings (minutes, structuring an agenda)
  • Write standard processes following instructions
  • Organize and conduct effective meetings

Emotional Intelligence

LO8
  • Introduction to emotional intelligence
  • Self-awareness and self-management
  • Social awareness and relationship management

Personal Development Planning

LO9
  • Conduct SWOT analysis
  • Develop strategies for personal growth and career development

Professional Etiquette

LO10
  • Professional, telephone, and email etiquettes (netiquette)
  • Identify professional jargon in telephone conversations
  • Develop listening and speaking skills for workplace communication
  • Construct effective professional conversations
  • Understand professional responsibility and code of ethics
  • Develop effective business writing and email correspondence skills

Research Skills

LO11
  • Identify the structure of a research paper
  • Demonstrate understanding of research concepts, design, and ethics
  • Review sample research papers and construct an abstract

Dining and Grooming Etiquette

LO12
  • Display professional behavior in formal settings
  • Critically discuss social decorum
  • Dress appropriately in professional contexts

Professional Skills Development Journey

Foundational Skills

Building core competencies in communication, professionalism, and workplace essentials (LO1-LO4)

Collaborative Skills

Developing leadership, teamwork, and interpersonal abilities (LO5-LO8)

Advanced Professionalism

Mastering research, etiquette, and personal development strategies (LO9-LO12)
